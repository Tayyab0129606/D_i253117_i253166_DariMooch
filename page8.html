About Dari Mooch

We're on a mission to elevate men's grooming with premium, natural products crafted for the modern gentleman.

Who We Are

Founded in 2018 in Karachi, Pakistan, Dari Mooch was born out of a simple frustration: premium-quality beard grooming products were either unavailable locally or outrageously expensive to import. Our founder, Ali Khan, decided to change that.

Our mission is straightforward — to provide every man in Pakistan with access to world-class beard grooming products at a fair price. We source only the finest natural ingredients: argan oil from Morocco, jojoba oil from the Sonoran Desert, and shea butter from West Africa.

Today, Dari Mooch serves over 10K+ happy customers across Pakistan, with a growing community of grooming enthusiasts who believe that taking care of your beard is an act of self-respect.

Our Core Values

Natural Ingredients

Every product is made with ethically sourced, 100% natural ingredients. No harsh chemicals, no compromises.

Integrity First

We're transparent about every ingredient, every price, and every decision we make as a business.

Sustainability

Our packaging is recyclable and our supply chain is designed with a minimal environmental footprint.
